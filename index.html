HOW TO PLAY THE PEXZZLES GAME (Instructions) üìë


1. Search for an Image

Enter a keyword in the search bar (e.g., "nature," "animals," or "city"). Use the dropdown menu to select a difficulty level: 3x3 (Easy) 4x4 (Medium) 5x5 (Hard), and click the "Search" button.

2. Select an Image

Click on the image you want to use for your puzzle. The puzzle will load, and the game begins.

3. Solve the Puzzle

Rearrange the shuffled pieces by dragging and dropping them into the correct positions. Use the background of each piece as a guide to recreate the original image.

4. Track Your Progress

Watch the timer to see how long it takes to complete the puzzle. The correct pieces counter updates in real-time to show how many pieces are in the right place.

5. Finish the Puzzle

Once all the pieces are correctly arranged, the puzzle is complete! Your time and difficulty level will be saved to the scoreboard.

6. Start a New Game

Click the "New Game" button to return to the search screen and play again.
